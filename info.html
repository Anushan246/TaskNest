Welcome to the Task Manager! 🚀

Get organized and boost your productivity.

➕ How to Add a Task

  • Go to the Add Task tab.
  • Enter your task, select its priority, and (optionally) mark it as important with a password.
  • Click Add Task 👉 to save it.

🗑️ How to Remove or Delete a Task

  • Click Delete ❌ next to a task to move it to the Deleted Tasks (Recycle Bin).
  • Important tasks require a password to delete 🔒.

♻ How to Restore a Task

  • Go to the Deleted Tasks tab 🗑️.
  • Click Restore ↻ next to a deleted task to bring it back to your active list.

⚙️ Other Features

  • Filter tasks by priority (High, Medium, Low) 📈.
  • Mark tasks as completed by clicking on them ✅.
  • See a summary of total, completed, and remaining tasks 📊.
  • Important tasks are protected and require a password to view or delete 🔒.
